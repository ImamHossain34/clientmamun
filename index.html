Samitivej Sukhumvit Hospital & Samitivej Srinakarin Hospital
1. Samitivej Sukhumvit Hospital

Location: Sukhumvit 49, Bangkok, Thailand
Established: 1979
Accreditation: JCI (Joint Commission International) accredited

Samitivej Sukhumvit Hospital is one of Thailand’s leading private hospitals, renowned for its advanced medical technology, international standards of care, and patient-centered services. It is part of the Samitivej Hospital Group, one of the largest healthcare networks in Thailand. The hospital is particularly popular among expatriates, international patients, and medical tourists, offering multilingual medical staff and interpreters.
Key Services & Specialties:
  • Comprehensive Medical Care: General medicine, internal medicine, and specialized treatments
  • Emergency & Trauma Center: 24/7 emergency services with advanced life-saving facilities
  • Women’s Health & Maternity Services: Obstetrics, gynecology, prenatal and postnatal care
  • Pediatrics & Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU): Leading pediatric care unit with international standards
  • Cancer Center: Advanced oncology services, including chemotherapy and targeted therapy
  • Orthopedics & Sports Medicine: Joint replacement, sports injuries, and rehabilitation
  • Cardiology & Cardiovascular Surgery: State-of-the-art heart care, including cardiac surgery
  • Gastroenterology & Digestive Disorders: Specialized treatments for digestive diseases
  • Liver & Pancreas Institute: Liver transplants and treatment of liver-related conditions
  • Cosmetic & Aesthetic Surgery: Plastic surgery, dermatology, and anti-aging treatments
  • Wellness & Preventive Medicine: Health check-ups, vaccinations, and corporate wellness programs
  • Medical Tourism Services: Dedicated international patient services, interpreters, and visa assistance

2. Samitivej Srinakarin Hospital

Location: Srinakarin Road, Bangkok, Thailand
Established: 1997
Accreditation: JCI accredited

Samitivej Srinakarin Hospital is a tertiary care hospital that provides specialized and advanced medical treatments. It is well-equipped with cutting-edge technology and serves both Thai and international patients, especially those from neighboring regions. This hospital is also known for its expertise in complex medical cases, trauma care, and mother-and-child services.
Key Services & Specialties:
  • Advanced Trauma & Emergency Care: One of the best trauma centers in Bangkok
  • Mother & Child Center: Comprehensive maternity and pediatric care, including high-risk pregnancies
  • Pediatric Specialty Center: Pediatric surgery, neonatology, and child development programs
  • Orthopedic & Joint Replacement Center: Hip, knee, and spinal surgery
  • Neuroscience Center: Treatment for stroke, epilepsy, and neurological disorders
  • Orthopedics & Sports Medicine: Joint replacement, sports injuries, and rehabilitation
  • Cardiology & Cardiovascular Surgery: State-of-the-art heart care, including cardiac surgery
  • Digestive Disease Center: Specialized care for gastrointestinal conditions
  • Heart Institute: Cardiac diagnostics, treatment, and surgery
  • Cancer Center: Comprehensive oncology services
  • Fertility & IVF Clinic: Assisted reproductive technology and fertility treatments
  • Rehabilitation & Physiotherapy: Post-surgical rehabilitation and sports therapy

Why Choose Samitivej Hospitals?

Both Samitivej Sukhumvit and Samitivej Srinakarin Hospitals provide:
  • Internationally accredited healthcare services (JCI accreditation)
  • Multilingual medical staff & patient support
  • Cutting-edge medical technology
  • Dedicated services for international patients and medical tourists
  • Comprehensive health check-up programs

These hospitals are preferred choices for expatriates, diplomats, and medical tourists due to their high standards of care, world-class facilities, and patient-friendly environment.
Phyathai 2 Hospital
Hospital Overview:

Location: Phaholyothin Road, Bangkok, Thailand
Established: 1987
Accreditation: JCI (Joint Commission International) accredited

Phyathai 2 Hospital is one of Thailand’s leading private hospitals, known for its high-quality healthcare services, advanced medical technology, and patient-centered care. The hospital is part of Phyathai Hospital Group, which operates under Bangkok Dusit Medical Services (BDMS)—one of Asia’s largest healthcare networks. Phyathai 2 Hospital is particularly popular among expatriates, international patients, and medical tourists, offering a wide range of specialized treatments with multilingual medical staff and international patient services.

Key Medical Services & Centers of Excellence:
Phyathai 2 Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services across multiple specialties, including:

1. Centers of Excellence:
  • Heart Center: Advanced cardiology services, including angioplasty, bypass surgery, and heart failure management.
  • Neurology & Neurosurgery Center: Specialized treatment for stroke, epilepsy, brain tumors, and spinal disorders.
  • Orthopedic Center: Expertise in joint replacement, spinal surgery, sports medicine, and trauma care.
  • Digestive Disease & Liver Center: Advanced endoscopy, colonoscopy, and liver disease management.
  • Oncology & Cancer Treatment Center: Comprehensive chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and minimally invasive cancer treatments.
  • Fertility & IVF Clinic: Assisted reproductive technology, including IVF, ICSI, and fertility preservation.
  • Women’s Health Center: Specialized services for gynecology, pregnancy care, and menopause management.
  • Pediatrics & Neonatal Care: High-standard pediatric and neonatal intensive care (NICU) services.
  • Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery: Aesthetic and reconstructive procedures, including cosmetic surgery and skin treatments.
  • Wellness & Preventive Medicine: Comprehensive health check-up programs, executive check-ups, and corporate wellness solutions.
2. International Patient Services:
Phyathai 2 Hospital has a dedicated International Services Department to assist patients from different countries. Services include:
  • Multilingual interpreters (English, Arabic, Chinese, Bangladeshi, etc.)
  • Medical coordination for visa processing
  • Airport pickup and concierge services
  • Customized treatment plans for international patients
Awards & Accreditations:
Phyathai 2 Hospital has received multiple awards and recognitions for its excellence in medical care, patient safety, and hospital management. Key achievements include:
  • JCI Accreditation (Joint Commission International): Recognized for meeting international healthcare standards.
  • HA (Hospital Accreditation, Thailand): Certified for high-quality hospital management and patient safety.
  • Global Healthcare Accreditation (GHA): Acknowledged for excellence in medical tourism services.
  • Best Hospital for Medical Tourism: Recognized for providing top-tier healthcare services to international patients.
  • Top Fertility Center Award: Acknowledged for excellence in assisted reproductive technology and IVF treatments.

Why Choose Samitivej Hospitals?

  • World-class medical services with internationally accredited standards.
  • Highly qualified specialists in various medical fields.
  • State-of-the-art technology and advanced diagnostic facilities.
  • Comprehensive patient care, from diagnosis to post-treatment support.
  • Multilingual support for seamless international patient experience.
  • Affordable healthcare solutions with high-quality treatment.

Phyathai 2 Hospital continues to be a preferred choice for both local and international patients, offering cutting-edge medical treatments, premium facilities, and excellent patient care.
